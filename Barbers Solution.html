About Us

Welcome to Barber's Solution – Your one-stop solution for booking a barber. Barber's Solution is a website that allows you to book appointments by choosing available time slots with your preferred barber. Our goal is to simplify the traditional haircutting process by automating the physical queues and transitioning to a digital appointment system. This website was developed as part of a college project to fulfill the preliminary requirements for the BICTE course at MRC Tahachal, affiliated with Tribhuvan University. The system currently has two user roles: User & Admin, each with role-based login and registration.

Key Features:
  • User & Admin Registration
  • User & Admin Login
  • View Available Barber Profiles
  • Choose Offered Services with Their Prices
  • Book Appointments
The idea for this project originated from a brainstorming session among the founders. As of now, there isn't a system that focuses on simplifying the appointment booking process for haircuts. So, the idea was embraced, and the product was launched.
